Freelance Virtual Assistant

Woski Group London(remote) - Mexico City (remote)
1 hour a day

Job Description

We are seeking a highly motivated and organized freelance virtual assistant to join our team for 1 hour a day, for 20 business days. The virtual assistant will be responsible for supporting our Senior Front End Engineer by taking notes during Zoom meetings, sending emails, and sending WhatsApp messages.

Responsibilities

  • Attend daily Zoom meetings with the Senior Front End Engineer and take detailed notes on discussions, decisions, and action items.
  • Send follow-up emails to meeting participants with meeting minutes and action items.
  • Draft and send emails on behalf of the Senior Front End Engineer to internal and external stakeholders.
  • Send WhatsApp messages to team members and external stakeholders as required.
  • Provide general administrative support to the Senior Front End Engineer as needed.

Experience

  • Experience using Zoom, WhatsApp, and email.
  • Ability to work independently and take initiative.
  • Strong organizational and time management skills.
  • Excellent verbal and written communication skills in English.

Benifits

  • Flexible working hours: As a freelance virtual assistant, you can choose your working hours that suit your schedule and lifestyle.
  • Work from anywhere: You can work remotely from anywhere in the world as long as you have a stable internet connection and necessary software tools.
  • Competitive pay: We offer competitive hourly rates based on your skills and experience. Starting at 20 USD per hour.
  • Stable work schedule: We hire on a monthly basis, so you can expect monthly work agreements.
